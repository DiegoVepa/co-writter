# LinkedIn Carousel - Framework/Educational Format

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## Purpose

Carousel posts (PDF slide format) for frameworks, step-by-step guides, and educational content. High engagement format, use 1-2x per month for pillar content.

## Structure Template

### Slide Count:
- **Minimum:** 5 slides
- **Optimal:** 6-8 slides
- **Maximum:** 10 slides

### Required Slides:

**Slide 1: Title/Hook**
- Eye-catching headline
- Subheadline or value proposition
- Your name/credential

**Slides 2-N: Content Slides**
- One main idea per slide
- 2-3 bullet points or short paragraphs
- Visual hierarchy (headline + supporting points)

**Final Slide: CTA/Summary**
- Key takeaway
- Call to action
- Link to profile or Substack

## Design Guidelines

### Visual Style:
- **Clean and minimal** (not overly designed)
- **Professional, not corporate** (avoid stock photo aesthetic)
- **Consistent color scheme** (2-3 colors max)
- **High contrast** (readable on mobile)

### Typography:
- **Headline:** Bold, 32-40pt
- **Body text:** 18-24pt
- **Spacing:** Generous white space

### Color Palette Options:

**Option 1: Professional Blue**
- Primary: #1A73E8 (Blue)
- Accent: #34A853 (Green)
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Text: #202124

**Option 2: Warm Minimalist**
- Primary: #FF6B35 (Orange)
- Accent: #004E89 (Navy)
- Background: #F7F7F7
- Text: #2C3E50

**Option 3: Tech Modern**
- Primary: #6366F1 (Indigo)
- Accent: #8B5CF6 (Purple)
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Text: #111827

## Content Frameworks by Theme

### "What I Built" Carousel

**Structure:**
1. Title: "How I Built [X] in [Y Time]"
2. The Problem
3. What I Tried First (failures)
4. The Solution
5. The Build (technical overview)
6. Results (before/after)
7. Key Lessons
8. CTA

**Example Outline:**
```
Slide 1: How I Cut Quote Turnaround from 2 Days to 20 Minutes
Slide 2: The Problem - Sales was waiting on product specs
Slide 3: What Didn't Work - Manual Airtable lookups
Slide 4: The Solution - AI-powered spec generator
Slide 5: The Build - n8n + Claude API + CRM integration
Slide 6: Results - 20min quotes, 34% faster close rate
Slide 7: Lessons - Integration matters more than the AI
Slide 8: CTA - What would you automate first?
```

---

### "Framework" Carousel

**Structure:**
1. Title: "[Framework Name]"
2. Why Most Approaches Fail
3. Step 1: [Action]
4. Step 2: [Action]
5. Step 3: [Action]
6. Common Mistakes
7. How to Get Started
8. CTA

**Example Outline:**
```
Slide 1: The AI Integration Framework
Slide 2: Why Most AI Pilots Fail (it's not the AI)
Slide 3: Step 1 - Map Your Manual Processes
Slide 4: Step 2 - Find the Boring Bottlenecks
Slide 5: Step 3 - Automate ONE Thing First
Slide 6: Common Mistakes (going too big, too fast)
Slide 7: Start Here - Pick Your First Process
Slide 8: CTA - Follow for more frameworks
```

---

### "Listicle" Carousel

**Structure:**
1. Title: "X Ways to [Achieve Outcome]"
2. Intro/Context
3. Item #1
4. Item #2
5. Item #3
6. Item #4 (if applicable)
7. Item #5 (if applicable)
8. Summary + CTA

**Example Outline:**
```
Slide 1: 5 Boring Automations That Save $10K/Year
Slide 2: For HVAC, Plumbing, and Trade Businesses
Slide 3: #1 - Appointment Reminders (save $2K)
Slide 4: #2 - Quote Follow-Ups (save $3K)

Slide 6: #4 - Review Requests (save $2K)
Slide 7: #5 - Missed Call Auto-Response (save $1.5K)
Slide 8: Total: $10K saved. Pick one to start.
```

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] 6-8 slides (not too short, not too long)
- [ ] Clear value proposition on Slide 1
- [ ] One main idea per slide
- [ ] High contrast, readable on mobile
- [ ] Consistent visual style across all slides
- [ ] No walls of text (max 3 bullets per slide)
- [ ] Final slide has clear CTA
- [ ] Caption previews the carousel content
- [ ] Engagement question in caption
- [ ] Passes employment safety filter
- [ ] Authentic voice (not generic LinkedIn spam)

---

## When NOT to Use Carousel

Don't force carousel format when:
- Insight can be expressed in text alone
- Content doesn't benefit from visual breakdown
- You're just trying to game engagement
- Content feels like recycled generic advice

**Remember:** Carousels should provide genuine value, not just packaging.
