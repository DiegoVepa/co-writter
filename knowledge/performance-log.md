### By Audience Segment

**Segment A (Career Pivot):**
- Target commenters: Product managers, marketing leaders, hiring managers
- Success signal: Comments asking about methodology or tools
- Red flag: Only engagement from other creators/consultants

**Segment B (Agency Build):**
- Target commenters: SMB owners, trades business owners
- Success signal: DMs asking for advice, shares to industry groups
- Red flag: Only tech/AI audience engaging

## Qualitative Signals

### Positive Signals to Track

- [ ] Comments from target audience (not just creators)
- [ ] Questions about methodology or tools
- [ ] DMs asking for advice
- [ ] Shares to industry groups
- [ ] Reposts with added commentary
- [ ] Connection requests from target profiles
- [ ] Mentions in others' posts

### Red Flags to Watch

- [ ] Only engagement from marketers/consultants
- [ ] Generic "great post!" comments
- [ ] No questions or discussion
- [ ] Declining engagement over time
- [ ] Wrong audience engaging
